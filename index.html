Do good while doing well

Bharat Parivartan is committed to overcome challenges of the traditional donation based philanthropy model through social investment opportunities. BP is creating a social entrepreneur fund to invest in socially relevant projects enabling entrepreneurs to work in a cohesive eco system. We believe in the three-tier approach with a clear intention.


group_work

Platform

Providing a platform to connect the social entrepreneurs and investors while providing tangible financial gains for the investor.

redeem

Investment

Allowing investors to have satisfaction of investing into wider cause of the society with more transparency of investments while providing access of capital to the social entrepreneurs.

done_all

Harmony

Creating opportunities for like-minded individuals to contribute with their expertise for being leaders in bringing about better societal harmony.


Bharat Parivartan will harness a growing number of investors who are expressing a desire to “do good while doing well". We will collaborate with impact investors and create opportunities for financial investments that produce social or environmental benefits.
